ENGAGEMENT OVERVIEW

Meridian Partners ("Client") has requested an operational due diligence assessment of Hartfield Precision Components ("Target") in connection with Meridian's proposed acquisition of a controlling interest at an estimated enterprise value of $62 million.

Webb Advisory Group ("Webb Advisory") proposes to conduct its standard management assessment and organizational health review as described below.

---

SCOPE OF WORK

Phase 1 — Document Review (Week 1–2)
• Review of organizational charts, reporting structures, job descriptions for senior roles
• Review of board meeting minutes (past 24 months)
• Analysis of management team compensation and incentive structure
• Review of any prior performance assessments or 360-degree feedback
• Analysis of turnover data and exit interview themes where available

Phase 2 — Management Interviews (Week 2–4)
• Structured behavioral and situational interviews with:
  - CEO / President
  - CFO
  - COO / VP Operations
  - VP Sales / Commercial Lead
  - 2–3 additional functional leaders identified during Phase 1
• Each interview: 60–90 minutes
• Interview protocol: Webb Advisory's proprietary framework (behavioral competencies + PE-readiness indicators)

Phase 3 — Reference & Background Validation (Week 3–4)
• Up to 8 reference calls (industry contacts, former colleagues, board members)
• Public background research on key leaders
• Comparison of stated credentials and track record against interview findings

Phase 4 — Assessment & Report (Week 4–6)
• Written assessment report (estimated 25–35 pages):
  - Executive Summary with overall risk rating
  - Individual leader profiles with competency ratings
  - Organizational health assessment
  - Integration risk flags (Red / Yellow / Green)
  - Recommended operating model adjustments post-close
• Management presentation of findings (2 hours, Meridian team + advisors)
• Available for follow-up Q&A during the 30 days post-delivery
