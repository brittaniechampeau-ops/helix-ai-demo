SLIDE 5 — OUR APPROACH
The Webb Method: Three-Layer Assessment

Layer 1 — Stated Capability
What the management team says they can do. (Verified against actual results.)

Layer 2 — Demonstrated Behavior
How decisions actually got made. Evidence from document review and reference calls.

Layer 3 — Transition Readiness
How this team will perform under PE ownership — reporting cadence, incentive alignment, change tolerance.

SLIDE 6 — CASE STUDY: OHIO MANUFACTURING CO.
Fund: Silverpoint Capital | Deal size: $47M EV | Close: 2024

Situation: Strong management team on paper — CFO with Big 4 background, COO from public company. Standard ODD would have cleared them.

What we found: COO's decision-making was fully consensus-driven. Nothing moved without full team buy-in. In a PE-backed acceleration environment this was a critical integration risk.

Result: Silverpoint restructured the management incentive package pre-close and embedded an operating partner from day one. Client reported it as one of their cleanest first-year integrations.
