## ONE-LINE POSITIONING

**Helix AI turns messy GTM strategy into structured execution context for marketing systems.**

---

## THE PROBLEM IN ONE PARAGRAPH

Enterprise marketing teams have a context translation problem. Strategy lives in PowerPoint decks, campaign briefs, ICP documents, and sales plays. Execution lives in Salesforce, HubSpot, Marketo, and CRM systems. The gap between those two worlds is filled by humans manually translating strategy into campaign metadata, audience segments, targeting rules, contact lists, and sales follow-up briefs. This process is slow (2–3 weeks per campaign), lossy (30–50% of strategic intent is lost in translation), and doesn't scale. The bottleneck isn't strategy quality or execution capability — it's the translation layer.

---

## CATEGORY

**GTM Context Engine** — a new category between strategy tools (Google Docs, PowerPoint, Notion) and execution systems (Salesforce, HubSpot, Marketo, Eloqua).

Helix is not:
- A content generation tool (not another AI writer)
- A CRM (not replacing Salesforce)
- A data enrichment vendor (not ZoomInfo)
- A campaign management platform (not Marketo)

Helix is the intelligence layer that translates strategy context into execution-ready data that CRM and MAP systems can consume.

---

## THE CORE INSIGHT

**Every CRM and MAP system is starving for context.**

Salesforce knows who your contacts are. It doesn't know why they matter for this campaign, which persona they map to, what offer they should receive, or where they sit in the buying committee. That context lives in a deck that no one ever connected to the CRM.

The problem is not a lack of data. Enterprise companies have enormous amounts of marketing data. The problem is that strategic context — the *why* behind a campaign — never makes it into the systems that run the campaign.

Helix closes that gap. Not by generating more content, but by extracting, structuring, and translating the context that already exists.
