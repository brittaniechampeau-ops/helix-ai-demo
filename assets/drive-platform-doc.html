The tools · Step-by-step
D
Tool 01
Discover
Platform Readiness Assessment — figures out which tools you need, in what order, and routes you there.
01
Enter org context
Name, industry, team size, revenue band, and primary growth challenge. Takes 60 seconds. This becomes the header of the readiness report.
02
Complete the readiness assessment
4 sections, 4 questions each — Taxonomy, Signal, Strategy, and Execution. Micro accounts skip Taxonomy and Signal (not relevant at that stage) and go straight to Strategy.
03
Review the readiness report
Scores across all four dimensions, readiness levels (Low / Medium / High), module recommendations, and a sequenced implementation roadmap.
04
Save to record and launch
"Save to record" writes the readiness scores to Supabase. The recommended module buttons link directly to each tool. Vector reads this record on login and surfaces the right track automatically.

R
Tool 02
Resolve
Taxonomy Workbench — builds the shared vocabulary that every other tool is built on top of.
01
Review default taxonomy
Resolve loads with a pre-seeded taxonomy based on 18 practice archetypes. Review the existing Audience, Offer, and Content categories.
02
Add, edit, and define terms
Click any term to edit it. Add new terms with the + button. Set each term to Defined (teal) or Pending (gray). Remove terms that don't fit your practice.
03
Add custom categories
Not seeing a category you need? Add Category creates a new column. Useful for industry-specific segmentation that the defaults don't cover.
04
Export to Vector
Export Taxonomy generates a JSON file formatted for Vector's taxonomy fields. Saves automatically to localStorage and syncs to your org's Supabase record — all team members see the same taxonomy.

I
Tool 03
Intelligence
Market Signals — extracts buyer intelligence from your existing documents so Vector's positioning is built on evidence, not assumptions.
01
Add your Anthropic API key
Click the API dot in the topbar to open the key banner. Paste your key — it's stored only in your browser's localStorage, never on a server.
02
Upload documents
Drag in discovery call transcripts (.vtt, .txt), pitch decks (.pptx), market research, competitive intel, or win/loss reports (.xlsx, .csv). All parsed in the browser.
03
Extract signals
Claude reads each document and extracts tagged signals: BUYER_SIGNAL, MARKET_SIGNAL, COMPETITIVE, TREND, PAIN, BUDGET. Each signal is quoted and sourced.
04
Signals flow into Vector
Intelligence saves a signal summary to localStorage that Vector reads during the AI pipeline step — so the positioning agents have real market evidence to work from.

V
Tool 04
Vector
Growth Command Center — builds your complete growth strategy across 8 steps, from diagnostic to 90-day activation plan.
01
Practice Diagnostic
8 questions about your firm — who you serve, what you offer, how you grow. Vector uses these to calibrate every subsequent output. Resolve taxonomy and Intelligence signals flow in automatically.
02
AI Pipeline runs 4 agents
Signal Extractor → Positioning Agent → Credibility Drafter → Offer Architect. Runs in under 60 seconds. All outputs are editable.
03
Positioning & Offers
Three fully-developed service offers with names, pricing tiers, framing, and activation plans. Regenerate any offer, or try a different angle.
04
Referral Engine
AI-drafted warm intro language for your top referral sources — matched to the right offer, ready to send. Not a contact list, actual language.
05
Credibility Kit, Client Intake & 90-Day Plan
AI-written case studies, a transcript-based intake tool that extracts deal signals before you write a proposal, and a phased 90-day activation roadmap with milestone targets.
06
Growth Command Center & Campaign DNA
A live operating dashboard — pipeline tracker, 90-day checklist, revenue targets. Campaign DNA maps your positioning to your taxonomy and generates import-ready payloads for HubSpot, Salesforce, Outreach, and LinkedIn.

E
Tool 05
Engine
Content Operations Metadata Engine — takes your Vector strategy and generates structured DAM metadata for every content asset across every recommended channel.
01
Content plan loads from Vector
Engine reads your Vector strategy (positioning, offers, channels) and your Resolve taxonomy from localStorage automatically. No re-entry needed.
02
Review recommended assets
Engine generates a content plan per channel from your Campaign DNA — Webinars, Slide Decks, LinkedIn Series, Email Sequences, Blog Articles, White Papers, One-Pagers, Partner Briefs.
03
Fill in asset metadata
Select an asset to open the metadata form. Asset-type-specific fields appear — DAM folder path, copy variants, subject lines, CTA text, audience tags, taxonomy mapping. Live JSON preview updates on the right.
04
Export all metadata
Export All Metadata downloads a CSV with every asset across every channel — ready to import into your DAM, CMS, or project management system.
